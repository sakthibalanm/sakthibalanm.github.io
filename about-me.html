About Me

After my doctorate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Madras in 2004, I worked as a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Computer Science, University of Western Ontario, London, Ontario, Canada until 2008. Then I worked in various capacities in Infosys Limited, Electronics City, Bangalore for seven years until May 2015. From June 2015 I am working as Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The LNM Institute of Information Technology, Jaipur, India.

For other interests please have a look at other pages.

I work in the broad areas of Web Science and Data Analytics. In specific, my interests include Social Network Analysis, Bias & Privacy in the Web and Action Recognition in Sports Videos. Recently I have also started exploring in the area of Music Information Retrieval (with respect to Indian Classical Music).
