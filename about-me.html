About Me

After my doctorate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Madras in 2004, I worked as a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Computer Science, University of Western Ontario, London, Ontario, Canada until 2008. Then I worked in various capacities in Infosys Limited, Electronics City, Bangalore for seven years until May 2015.

I am also a trained Carnatic vocal musician. I have got training under Palghat Shri K V Narayanaswamy and Shri T K Subramaniam for more than 15 years. I did my arangetram in 1995 and have performed in various places in and around Tirunelveli. I have also performed in Tiruvayuru Thyagaraja Aradhana in 1990.

I am avid reader of books especially on philosophy, brain, mind, thinking, cognition, emotion, abstraction, and related stuffs. I blog here and here that include rants from all topics. Some of my favorite authors are M. Minsky, N. Frijda, V. S. Ramachandran, Steven Pinker, Douglas Hofstadter

My present research interest is in Social media analytics where I work in both structural and qualitative analysis.
